Here is a simple and delicious salmon recipe that is cooked in the oven in aluminum foil and is suitable for a ketogenic diet:

Recipe description

This salmon has a tender and flaky texture, with a delicate and moist interior and a crisp and flavorful exterior.
The bright and refreshing flavor of the dill and lemon pairs perfectly with the rich and savory taste of the salmon, creating a well-balanced and satisfying dish.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 sprigs fresh dill
  • 4 slices lemon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Place each salmon fillet on a piece of aluminum foil. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Top each fillet with a sprig of dill and a slice of lemon.
  4. Fold the foil over the salmon to form a sealed packet. Place the packets on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  6. Serve the salmon with your choice of sides.

Remarks

This recipe should yield about 4 servings. Each serving has approximately 200 calories, 13 grams of fat, 9 grams of carbohydrates, and 15 grams of protein.
The net carbs per serving are about 5 grams, which should help keep you in ketosis as long as you are maintaining your daily carbohydrate intake at or below your personal carb limit.
